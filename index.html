    Our Story

    It all began with a hat. Patrick and Hannah first met at a Georgetown Greek mixer in the winter of 2010. He was from the University of Maryland, and she was from the University of Virginia. Hannah was skeptical and unimpressed by Patrick, but intrigued. After the party, Hannah requested to take Patrick's hat. Although their initial meeting was unremarkable, they always kept in touch, until Patrick decided to retrieve his hat back from UVA.

    From there, Hannah and Patrick became inseparable. Even the blizzard of 2010 couldn't keep them apart. From College Park to Charlottesville, from North Potomac to West Chester and Virginia Beach they would always visit each other at every opportunity. Whether it was stay at home TV/Movie night, a trip downtown, or a vacation abroad, Hannah and Patrick always enjoyed each others company.

    Engagement

    After dating for almost seven years, Patrick popped the question on the last day of their vacation in Rome, Florence, and Venice. Patrick originally planned to propose on a gondola along the beautiful canals of Venice. However, Hannah booked an atypical excursion where they learned how to row the gondola instead. Hannah had difficulty rowing the gondola and was left frustrated, so Patrick decided not to propose at that time.

    Luckily, Hannah wanted to visit the San Giorgio Maggiore bell tower to see the sunset. The bell tower overlooked all of Venice and the sky was absolutely stunning! As the sun set and the crowd started thinning out, Patrick got down on one knee and asked the question!
